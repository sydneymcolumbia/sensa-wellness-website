Why We Built Sensa

We didn't build a test kit.
We built a warning system.

Every person on this team has watched someone they love suffer from a condition that started as invisible, silent inflammation. It became something medicine could no longer fix. We refused to accept that as inevitable.

The Problem We Couldn't Ignore

Disease doesn't begin with
a diagnosis.

It begins years, sometimes decades, earlier, as chronic low-grade inflammation quietly damages tissues, disrupts hormones, and derails the body's natural ability to regulate itself. By the time a doctor names it (heart disease, diabetes, autoimmune disorder, cancer), the window for simple, powerful intervention has often already closed.

This is the reality that drives us. Not the treatment of late-stage disease, but the disruption of the chain that leads there. Inflammation is the root. It is the origin. And it is measurable, if you have the right tool.

Until now, checking your inflammation required a doctor's order, a lab visit, days of waiting, and a bill that most people can't afford to repeat. We asked: what if it was as easy as a pregnancy test? What if you could know today, at home, in minutes?

Why This Matters

Catch it early. Change everything.

The difference between catching inflammation now and catching the disease it causes later is not just clinical. It's life-altering.

○

Reversibility

Inflammation in its early stages responds powerfully to lifestyle changes: diet, sleep, movement, stress management. These same changes have little effect on diseases that have already taken hold. The earlier you know, the more options you have.

△

Agency

Knowledge is the first requirement of action. Most people living with elevated inflammation have no idea, not because they don't care about their health, but because no one ever gave them a way to check. Sensa changes that equation permanently.

■

Prevention over treatment

Every late-stage diagnosis represents a prevention failure. Not a moral failure. A systemic one. A healthcare system that only reacts to crisis will always lose to the quiet, compounding nature of chronic disease. We're building the tools that tip that balance.

◆

Equity

Proactive health screening has historically belonged to people with premium insurance, time, and access. At $49, Sensa puts clinically-informed inflammation monitoring within reach for anyone, regardless of their zip code or coverage.

○

Continuous awareness

A single blood test is a snapshot. Inflammation fluctuates with stress, diet, sleep, and illness. Regular monitoring with Sensa gives you a pattern. Patterns are where real insight lives. One test tells you something. Twelve tell you a story.

△

Trust through science

We didn't create a wellness gadget. We built a clinically-grounded, validated tool that measures C-reactive protein (the same biomarker used in hospital labs) and puts that data in your hands in minutes. Science first. Simplicity second.

How We Built It

From a question to a
real answer.

Sensa wasn't born in a boardroom. It was born from frustration, a shared feeling among our founders that the tools to understand chronic inflammation existed in labs and clinics, but had never been made available to the people who needed them most.

Building a reliable, saliva-based CRP test that works at home took years of research, iteration, and validation. We weren't willing to ship something that couldn't stand behind its results. The science had to be right before anything else.

We paired that with an app that doesn't just display your number. It helps you understand what it means, what's driving it, and what you can do about it. Because a number without context is just noise.

The question that started everything

Team members with personal experience of inflammation-driven illness came together around one shared question: why is there no simple, affordable way to monitor this at home?

Building the science

We developed and validated a saliva-based lateral flow assay capable of detecting CRP at clinically meaningful thresholds, without needles, labs, or waiting rooms.

The Sensa app

We built a companion app that logs your results over time, identifies patterns, and gives you science-backed guidance on the lifestyle factors most likely to be driving your levels.

Now available — for everyone

Sensa is live. The test that used to require a clinic visit, a requisition form, and a two-day wait now ships to your door and delivers results in under five minutes.
