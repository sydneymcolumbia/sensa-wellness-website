We are on a mission to make inflammation monitoring a daily habit for everyone. We are looking for partners and people who share that vision.

Partnerships

Let's Build Something Together

We partner with organizations that are as serious about preventive health as we are. If your work brings you close to people who care about their long-term wellbeing, there is likely a fit.

Healthcare Providers and Clinics

Recommend Sensa to patients who would benefit from tracking their inflammation at home between visits. We offer provider resources, bulk pricing, and co-branded materials.

Corporate Wellness Programs

Bring inflammation monitoring to your workforce. Chronic inflammation costs employers billions in lost productivity and healthcare spending. We offer group pricing and program support for companies of all sizes.

Research Institutions

We are interested in partnering with academic and research organizations studying inflammation, chronic disease, and preventive health. Contact us to discuss collaboration opportunities.

Wellness Coaches and Personal Trainers

Help your clients understand what is happening inside their bodies, not just on the outside. Sensa gives coaches a concrete biomarker to track alongside fitness and nutrition progress.

Influencers and Content Creators

If you create content around health, longevity, biohacking, or wellness and have an engaged audience, we would love to talk about how we can work together.

Senior Living and Assisted Care

Older adults are among those who benefit most from regular inflammation monitoring. We offer tailored programs for senior living communities, assisted care facilities, and elder care organizations.
