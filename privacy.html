Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Sensa ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information when you use the Sensa mobile application ("App").

Wellness use only. Sensa is a general wellness application. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or manage any disease or medical condition. The wellness scores generated by the App are not clinical measurements and should not be used as a substitute for professional medical advice.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: email address and display name provided when you create an account.
  • Wellness scan data: the wellness score (0–100), wellness label, and timestamp recorded each time you perform a scan. Raw colorimetric measurements used to derive your score are also stored to support trend analysis.
  • Scan images: photos of your test vial captured during a scan are stored locally on your device only. They are not uploaded to our servers.
  • Usage data: standard crash reports and app performance data collected automatically by Firebase.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Display your wellness scores, trends, and history within the App.
  • Send you optional scan reminders you configure in the App.
  • Generate a wellness report you can export and share at your discretion.
  • Improve the performance and reliability of the App.

We do not use your data for advertising. We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your account and scan data are stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore), a cloud database service provided by Google LLC. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Scan images are stored only on your local device and are never transmitted to our servers.

Access to your data is restricted to your account. We do not share your individual scan data with any third party without your explicit consent.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase: authentication, database, and crash reporting (Firebase Privacy Policy).
  • Google Fonts: typefaces loaded from Google's CDN.

5. Health Data

Sensa generates a general wellness score derived from a colorimetric analysis of a test strip. This score is a proprietary lifestyle metric and is not a clinical measurement of any biomarker. We do not integrate with Apple Health, Google Fit, or any other health platform, and we do not share wellness data with healthcare providers or insurers.

6. Data Retention

Your account and scan data are retained for as long as your account remains active. You may delete individual scans within the App at any time. To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at the email address below and we will process your request within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Sensa is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
