# Sensa Wellness — Social Media Marketing Strategy

## Brand Voice
- Bold, direct, empowering (never alarmist)
- Science made accessible — not clinical, not dumbed-down
- Community-first: "we're in this together," not "buy this"
- Tone references: "Friends don't gatekeep wellness" / "Your Health, Decoded"

## Platform Strategy

### Instagram
**Goal:** Brand awareness + product discovery
**Audience:** Health-conscious adults 25–45, biohackers, chronic illness community, wellness enthusiasts

**Posting cadence:**
- Feed posts: 4x/week
- Stories: Daily (polls, tips, BTS)
- Reels: 2x/week

**Content types:**
- "Your Health, Decoded" squares — education/stat posts (existing series, keep going)
- "Why Test?" infographic style — icon-grid format (new)
- Promotional banners — bundle deals, seasonal sales (existing "Social Graphics" style)
- Reels: "Did you know?" short-form, product unboxings, result walkthroughs

### LinkedIn
**Goal:** Credibility, B2B partnerships, investor/media awareness
**Audience:** Healthcare professionals, corporate wellness buyers, investors, media

**Posting cadence:** 2–3x/week

**Content types:**
- Research-backed inflammation stats (adapted from IG content)
- Founder/team story posts
- Preventive health thought leadership
- Partnership and milestone announcements

## New Campaign Ideas

### 1. "Know Your Number" Campaign
**Concept:** Everyone knows their blood pressure, cholesterol, weight. Nobody knows their CRP. Change that.
**Tagline:** "Know Your Number. Know Your Risk."
**Assets:** Stat-heavy posts, comparison graphics, banner ads

### 2. "Before It Hurts" Campaign
**Concept:** Proactive testing — not reactive medicine.
**Tagline:** "Test before symptoms. Track before disease."
**Assets:** Before/after wellness posts, testimonials

### 3. Seasonal Hooks
| Season | Hook |
|--------|------|
| January | "This year, track what actually matters." (New Year health goals) |
| Spring/Allergy | "Seasonal allergies = inflammation in overdrive. Know your baseline." |
| Summer | "Heat + stress = higher CRP. Are you tracking?" |
| Fall | "Flu season isn't just about getting sick. Your inflammation score matters." |
