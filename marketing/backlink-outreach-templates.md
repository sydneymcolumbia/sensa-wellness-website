## Target Outreach List (Suggested)

### Wellness Blogs
- Search Google for "best wellness blogs" and pitch to top 20
- Search for "inflammation blog" and "anti-inflammatory diet blog"
- Look for bloggers who write about weight loss plateaus, GLP-1 alternatives

### Health Directories
- Healthline contributor/product pages
- Verywell Health resource lists
- WebMD community resources
- Local health resource directories

### University Programs
- Harvard Innovation Labs, i-lab
- MIT Sandbox, Martin Trust Center
- Boston-area public health programs
- University wellness centers

### Podcasts
- Search Apple Podcasts for "wellness," "inflammation," "biohacking," "weight loss"
- Target podcasts with 500-5,000 listeners (more likely to respond than mega-shows)

### Press
- Local Boston media (Boston Globe, Boston Magazine, BostInno)
- Health tech publications (MobiHealthNews, Digital Health Today)
- Startup/innovation press (TechCrunch Health, Crunchbase News)
