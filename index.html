Traditional inflammation testing means blood draws, lab visits, and waiting days for results. Sensa uses salivary biomarkers—the same markers researchers use to study systemic inflammation—so you get actionable insight from a simple spit sample, right at home.

Why Choose Sensa

Health monitoring should be simple, affordable, and available to everyone.

Easy to Use

A saliva-based test that anyone can do. Non-invasive and safe for pregnant women, diabetics, children, and the elderly—no training required.

Cost Effective

No refrigeration, no batteries, no lab equipment. Each single-use device is shelf-stable at room temperature—designed to keep costs low without cutting corners on accuracy.

Built to Scale

From rural clinics to urban households, Sensa works anywhere. Integrates with our companion app so you can track trends and share results with your doctor.

How Sensa Works

Three steps. A few minutes. Real insight into what's happening inside your body.

1

Provide a Saliva Sample

Just spit into a Sensa device. No blood draws, no swabs, no discomfort whatsoever.

2

Wait for the Color Change

The device reacts with inflammatory biomarkers in your saliva. Within minutes, a visible color change appears.

3

Read Your Results

Open the Sensa app, scan your device, and see your inflammation level—with guidance on what it means.

Lightly Inflamed
Moderately Inflamed
Extremely Inflamed

Moderate or severe results? We'll recommend you follow up with your healthcare provider. Mild inflammation is often manageable through lifestyle changes—better sleep, nutrition, stress reduction, and movement.
